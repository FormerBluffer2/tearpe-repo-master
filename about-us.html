About Us

Legal Advisor - Web Dabbler - All Around Good Guy

I am an attorney with experience in a wide range of general corporate areas. My experience has been primarily with technology based companies, including pharmaceuticals and medical devices, and includes Fortune 500 senior management positions. Over time, I have discovered that more and more issues arise, either directly or indirectly, from areas that involve computer technology regardless of the industry. As a result, I have begun teaching myself computer coding as a way to improve my understanding of these issues as well as for personal enrichment.

In addition to my professional background, I have a wide range of interests that vary from playing hockey to volunteering with an organization that provides recreational opportunities for people with disabilities.

Experience - Versatility - Creativity

I have senior management experience with expertise in developing and implementing complex business and legal strategies. As Vice President and General Counsel, I was responsible for the general legal affairs of a fully integrated billion-dollar division of a Fortune 500 company with over 4500 employees. This included supervising a diverse staff of attorneys, patent attorneys, paralegals and support staff. Our department had the versatility to successfully implement common goals and develop positive working relationships with individuals in a wide range of roles and at all levels within the organization. My experience includes a broad range of general corporate, commercial and SEC matters, as well as the creativity to develop and implement sophisticated intellectual property strategies. Currently, I am available on a consulting or contract basis.

I went to law school at the University of Illinois College of Law in Champagne, Illinois, where I graduated Cum Laude. As an undergraduate, I went to Northern Illinois University where I graduated Magna Cum Laude with a degree in Accounting. Although I never practiced as an accountant, I did successfully pass both the Certified Public Accounting (CPA) and Certificate in Management Accounting (CMA) Examinations. I have found that my accounting background has been particularly helpful working in a corporate environment.

Although I don't have formal education in any of the following areas, I have had an interest in these areas for as long as I can remember:

  • Engineering - If I had to do it over (and my high school grades were better), I might go to school to become a mechanical engineer. I've always been curious to learn about new technologies and to understand how things work.
  • Astronaut - I still have hopes of one day being an astronaut and am excited about the renewed interest in space travel.

I am always open to learn new things. That is one of the reasons I am taking the time to build this website. Even though I am not likely to ever become an expert computer coder, I hope I will be able to learn enough to build a website that can become a useful resource. I am always open to constructive feedback, so please don't hesitate to let me know what you think of this website. In particular, I would be happy to receive any suggested changes or additions. Thank you.
