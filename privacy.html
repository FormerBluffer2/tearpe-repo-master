Security

We take precautions to protect your information. When you submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both online and offline.

Wherever we collect sensitive information, that information is encrypted and transmitted to us in a secure way. You can verify this by looking for a lock icon in the address bar and looking for "https" at the beginning of the address of the Web page.

You have discretion whether to provide this additional information and have the option to not provide any information that is not designated as required. Also, you are not required to register if you don't want to access Restricted Areas.

Cookies

We may use "cookies" on this site. A cookie is a piece of data stored on your hard drive to help us improve your access to our site and identify repeat visitors. Cookies can enable us to identify whether you have already logged into the website thus saving you the time required to log in repeatedly. Cookies can also track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on our site. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information on our site. Some of our business partners may use cookies on our site (for example, advertisers). However, we have no access to or control over these cookies.

This website contains links to other websites. By clicking on these links, you will be transferred to the website of the 3rd party. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these other sites. We encourage you to be aware when you leave our site and to read the privacy statements of any other site that you visit.

Even after you have completed your access to this website, we may still retain the data specified in this website for the regular operation of the site and as may be necessary to comply with legal requirements, etc.

Modification of this Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be modified from time to time without notice to you. The most current version of this privacy policy as posted on this website supersedes all previous versions. It is your responsibility to check periodically to determine whether a new version is in effect.
